I love the dark because it feels like the world finally stops demanding things from me. When the sun goes down, the frantic energy of the day—the noise, the expectations, and the constant visual clutter—simply dissolves into a quiet, velvet stillness. In the dark, the boundaries of the physical world blur, making room for thoughts to expand and imagination to take the lead. It’s a sanctuary of anonymity where I can just be without being watched or judged.
